Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Clear, honest answers to the questions our patients ask most.

What are Global Care Hospital's visiting hours?
+
Visiting hours are 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM – 7:00 PM daily. ICU and special-care units have restricted visiting; please check with the nursing station. Emergency wards are accessible 24/7 for immediate family.
Does Global Care accept health insurance?
+
Yes, we are empanelled with all major insurance companies including Star Health, HDFC Ergo, ICICI Lombard, New India Assurance, and government schemes like PMJAY and Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY).
How do I book an appointment?
+
You can book via our website contact form below, call +91 20 1234 5678, or visit our front desk. Online booking is available 24/7 and you will receive a confirmation within 30 minutes during business hours.
Where is Global Care Hospital located?
+
Our main campus is at Plot 45, Baner Road, Pune, Maharashtra 411045. We also operate satellite OPD centres in Nashik, Aurangabad, and Kolhapur for specialist consultations. All centres are well connected by public transport.
Is Global Care Hospital NABH accredited?
+
Yes, Global Care Hospital holds full NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals) accreditation, confirming our adherence to the highest national standards of patient safety, clinical outcomes, and hospital management.
Are treatments at Global Care affordable?
+
Absolutely. We offer transparent pricing, EMI options, and participate in several government health schemes to ensure that no patient is denied care due to financial constraints. Our social welfare team assists patients in need.
